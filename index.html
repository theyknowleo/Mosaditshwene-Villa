About the Villa

MosadiTshwene Villa is a luxury retreat in Kanye, Botswana — born from the inspiring journey of Neo Seate, a seasoned security adviser at Debswana and an award-winning horticulture farmer. Featured in newspapers and TV shows, she supplies retailers like Pick n Pay and Food Lovers Market while raising a beautiful family. This villa, rooted in her values of Growth, Endurance, and Versatility, offers a tranquil yet elegant escape for guests seeking comfort, connection, and celebration.

Rooms

The villa features 4 en-suite bedrooms, including a master suite with a walk-in closet. Each upstairs room has a private balcony. Guests enjoy a sunken lounge, private lounge, pyjama lounge, office, a fully equipped kitchen, double garage, and a stunning upstairs balcony/garden perfect for events. A pool is coming soon.
